## üåï Step 3: What is "Lua"?
When we write code in Roblox, we are speaking a language called **Lua**.

* **It means "Moon":** In Portuguese, Lua means Moon! üåë
* **It's a Translator:** Computers don't speak English. Lua is the "translator" that lets you tell the computer, *"Hey! Make that character jump!"* and the computer understands.
* **Why Lua?** It is famous for being fast and easy to learn. It's the perfect first language for a pro coder.

## üö¶ Your First Mission: The "Hello World" Test
Let's make sure your computer is listening to you!

1.  Open Roblox Studio and pick the **Baseplate** template.
2.  In the **Explorer** window (on the right), look for a folder called **ServerScriptService**.
3.  Click the white **(+)** and add a **Script**.
4.  Delete the text that is already there and type this: 
    ```lua
    print("I am a game developer!")
    ```
5.  Press the blue **Play** button at the top. 
6.  Look at your **Output** window. Did it say your message? 

**If you see that message, you just wrote your first line of Lua code! You are ready for Lesson 1.**
